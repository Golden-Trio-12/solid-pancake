RETRO-STYLE PING PONG


Instructions

HOW TO PLAY: Turn on the computer webcam and stand a few feet away from the camera so both wrists are fully visible in the screen. You need motion visiblity for this to work. Using your right wrist, control the position of the ping pong paddle. You will use it to hit the ping pong ball across the screen to the other side. Continue rallying and see how far you get!
